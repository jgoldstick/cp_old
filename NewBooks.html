Communicate With Your Child

PLAY TO TALK - A PRACTICAL GUIDE TO HELP YOUR CHILD JOIN THE CONVERSATION

Dr. Jim and COMMUNICATING PARTNERS Announces AN EXCITING, EASY. AND LIVELY NEW BOOK FOR YOU PLAY TO TALK A PRACTICAL GUIDE TO HELP YOUR CHILD JOIN THE CONVERSATION JAMES D. MAC DONALD, PhD and PAM STOIKA, PhD

Who is it for? Any parent or professional who wants to help a nonverbal or verbal child become more social and communicative.

Where does it come from? Over 30 years of clinical research at the Ohio State University and the Communicating Partners Center and Several years of practical application to many parents and professionals in Wisconsin by IDS (Integrated Developmental Services) a family-based clinical training program.

Who was it developed with? Over 100 families with typically developing children AND "LATE-TALKING CHILDREN as well as ones with: INFANTS AT RISK, AUTISM, PDD, ASD, DOWN SYNDROME, MOTOR DISORDERS (Apraxia, Neurological issues, Cerebral palsy), EMOTIONAL AND BEHAVIORAL CONDITIONS, and other "LATE TALKING CONDITIONS.

How can I get it? Play to talk is published by KIDDO PUBLISHING, which is a new company devoted to family and community based programs for children. This is their first book after publishing a number of exciting musical DVDs promoting community involvement with all children. To order see www.kiddopublishing.com or write IDS, 6506 Schroeder Road, Madison, Wi. 53711 Phone: 800-218-3781

For clinical services related to Play to Talk contact IDS (above) or Dr. 'Jim" MacDonald at Communicating Partners. www.jamesdmacdonald.org

Here is what is in PLAY TO TALK And what it can do for you.

PARENTS

  1. It describes the background, purposes and research support for Communicating Partners in 'real 'people" language.
  2. It teaches you five life-long strategies proven to help children become more Interactive, Communicative, Verbal, and Conversational and well behaved.
  3. It shows you how to use the ARM, which is a 'road-map' for you and your child to follow him or her from first movements to full conversations.
  4. You will be able to make your own "Family Practice Plan" and be in charge of your child's development.
  5. Shows you how to help your chill communicate in People play, Object Play, Literacy activities, and Daily life.
  6. Shows you how the ways you communicate can help reduce your child's misbehavior and help him be more civil and empathetic.
  7. Shows you how to help your child develop in daily activates from 'waking up' to 'going to bed". Every interaction can help a child communicate!

Play to Talk can be your helpful 'side-kick' in communicating with others for many years.

What play to Talk does for THERAPISTS, EDUCATORS, HOME PROVIDERS, ETC.

  1. Provides an evidenced based curriculum for early social and communicative development.
  2. Provides training for those not prepared to work with preverbal, socially different, non-conversational and behaviorally challenged children.
  3. Provides a tested model for collaborating with parents.
  4. Provides practical guides for making classrooms more social and communicative.
